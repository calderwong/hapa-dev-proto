# RPG Character Profile Design - Mimi Phan

## Design Philosophy

**Visual Language**: Dark fantasy RPG interface inspired by games like Diablo, World of Warcraft, and Final Fantasy XIV. Using rich, deep colors with golden accents to convey expertise and mystique. The design evokes the feeling of discovering a legendary character's profile in an ancient tome or magical interface.

**Color Palette**:
- Primary: Deep midnight blue (#0f1419) and rich purple (#2d1b69)
- Secondary: Warm gold (#f59e0b) and amber (#d97706) for highlights
- Accent: Mystical teal (#0891b2) and silver (#e5e7eb)
- Text: Crisp white (#ffffff) and soft gray (#d1d5db)

**Typography**:
- Display: "Cinzel" - Elegant serif for headings, evoking ancient manuscripts
- Body: "Inter" - Clean, readable sans-serif for content
- UI: "JetBrains Mono" - Monospace for stats and technical details
- Magical: Custom runic symbols and glyphs for decorative elements

## Visual Effects & Libraries

**Used Libraries**:
- Anime.js - Smooth character stat animations and UI transitions
- ECharts.js - Skill tree visualizations and progress bars
- p5.js - Magical particle effects and background animations
- Typed.js - Character dialogue and lore text reveals
- Splitting.js - Letter-by-letter text animations for spell effects
- Pixi.js - Advanced visual effects for magical elements
- Matter.js - Physics-based animations for floating UI elements

**Effects**:
- Floating magical particles and energy orbs
- Glowing skill nodes with pulsing animations
- Character portrait with subtle breathing animation
- Typewriter effect for character backstory
- Smooth stat bar animations with magical glow
- Interactive skill tree with connection lines
- Hover effects with magical aura and tooltips

## Character Profile Structure

**Character Sheet Layout**:
1. **Character Header**: Name, title, level, and portrait with magical frame
2. **Core Attributes**: Primary stats (INT, WIS, CHA, DEX, CON, STR)
3. **Skill Trees**: Organized by domain (AI Research, Data Science, Spirituality, etc.)
4. **Abilities & Spells**: Special techniques and methodologies
5. **Equipment & Tools**: Technologies and platforms mastered
6. **Quest Log**: Current projects and achievements
7. **Reputation**: Social presence and community standing
8. **Companions**: AI agents and collaborative projects

**Interactive Elements**:
- Clickable skill nodes that reveal detailed information
- Hover tooltips showing skill descriptions and requirements
- Expandable sections for detailed project information
- Filterable skill trees by category and level
- Character comparison mode
- Progress tracking for ongoing quests
